# GRIIT — Build Discipline Daily

## Subtitle
Challenge yourself. Build streaks. Level up.

## Description
GRIIT is the discipline-building app that turns self-improvement into a game. Join structured challenges like 75 Hard, daily cold showers, reading goals, and custom challenges — then prove your commitment with daily check-ins.

**Build unbreakable streaks**
Complete your daily tasks and secure each day. Watch your streak grow from 1 to 7 to 30 to 100+ days. Miss a day? Use a Streak Freeze or Last Stand to protect your progress.

**Prove it with verification**
No honor system here. Complete tasks with photo proof, built-in timers, GPS-tracked runs, and journal entries. Every day secured is earned.

**Level up your discipline**
Start as a Starter, progress through tiers as you build consistency. Earn achievements, hit milestones, and climb the leaderboard.

**Join the community**
See what others are accomplishing. Follow friends, join team challenges, and hold each other accountable.

**Premium features**
- Unlimited active challenges
- Streak Freeze protection
- Last Stand recovery
- Custom challenge creation
- Detailed discipline analytics

Download GRIIT and start building the discipline that changes everything.
